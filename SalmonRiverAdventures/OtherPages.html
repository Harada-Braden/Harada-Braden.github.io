Pricing

Prices include tax, meals, gear, and guide expenses. All you need to do is bring yourself and/or your party and we provide the rest. We will even drop you off at the launch site and pick you up at the takeout point so you can ease your mind and just enjoy the ride!


Short Adventure:

Actual river time is about 2 to 2.5 hours. It is a good idea to budget 3.5 to 4 hours of your time for this trip.

  • 1-6 people, $80 (per person)
  • 7-15 people, $70 (per person)
  • 16 or more people, $60 (per person)


Day Adventure:

Actual river time is about 8 hours. It is a good idea to budget 10 hours of your time for this trip. There will be 2 short stops, one stop will be for a meal which is provided and included in the price of the ticket.

  • 1-4 people, $200 (per person)
  • 5-9 people, $180 (per person)
  • 10 or more people, $150 (per person)

2-day, 1 night Camp-out:

This is your get-a-way for a couple days. Expect to show up at our location around 9am and leave at about 6pm the next day. We will provide 4 meals which include: lunch and dinner on the first day, breakfast and lunch on the second day. We provide all of the necessary equipment for rafting, camping, and having a great experience.

  • 1-4 people, $350 (per person)
  • 5-8 people, $325 (per person)
  • 8 or more people, $300 (per person)

Beginner Adventure

For the beginner we have the North Fork of the Salmon River. It is a slow-paced course with some rapids scattered throughout your trip. This is the trip to take if you want to just enjoy the scenery and get a quick rush for a couple hours.
